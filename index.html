About Abbas Kiarostami



"Here he is, one of the greatest of us all, the originator and master of the minimalist epic, the visionary who has raised the cinema of humanity to an unprecedented level of purity, the reluctant teacher who modestly confronts you with truths so profound that they will blind you with their luminous clairvoyance, the outrageous provocateur with an exhilarating capacity to make statements about his ideas and methods at which you will be so shocked that you will likely howl out loud, albeit joyfully."
Mike Leigh

Abbas Kiarostami was a pioneering Iranian filmmaker, photographer, and poet, recognized for his profound influence on contemporary Iranian cinema. Born on June 22, 1940, in Tehran, Kiarostami gained international acclaim in the 1990s with "Close-Up," known for his minimalist style and exploration of existential themes and human relationships.
He revolutionized storytelling with unconventional narrative structures, long takes, and naturalistic performances, often blending fiction and reality. His films, rich in universal themes like love and loss, used subtle gestures to evoke deep emotional responses.
Kiarostami's work extended to photography and poetry, integrating his visual and literary passions into his films. culture. His innovative approach and commitment to the human condition have left a lasting impact on global cinema, inspiring filmmakers worldwide. He passed away on July 4, 2016, but his legacy endures through his influential body of work. (Paraphrase: https://www.bookey.app/quote-author/abbas-kiarostami )

Knowledge Organization

After selecting the area of study and the 10 representative items, sourced from various institutions like libraries, archives, and museums, the initial phase of their analysis involves the Knowledge Organization Process. In this stage, the gathered data are systematically described and modeled, capturing different degrees of abstraction.

Conceptual Map

The Conceptual Map offers an initial overview of the descriptive process, illustrating all the data derived from the various items in natural language. It clearly outlines the relationships between these items and the central concept.

E/R Model


The Conceptual Map is converted into an E/R Model, a visual depiction that enables a more abstract description of the data. In this model, people, places, dates, and subjects are represented as Entities, while their interconnections are depicted as Relationships.

Metadata Analysis

The next phase involves revisiting the institutions and item providers to conduct a detailed analysis of the metadata they use for description. This entails identifying the standards and guidelines they adhere to. For those without a clear declaration, we used either the most commonly adopted standard for that type of institution or a more general metadata standard deemed appropriate for the specific provider.
